Audius is the ultimate streaming music player. Create playlist, no login required, stored in the indexDB in your browser. Share your playlists as hyperlinks, files and in Matrix rooms. Queue songs, play history. Organize playlists with drag and drop. Supports: YouTube/Vimeo/.mp3/.oga/.wav/.mp4/.webm/.ogg URLs. Runs "offline" - a pure HTML5 app that can run without a server. Plays Imgur "Most Viral" as video stream. Create your own web channels with the build in web scraper. Set start end times of media items (e.g. to cut of trailing silence) Split YouTube full albums into separate songs. Connect to the Matrix network and create rooms to share and discover music with your friends.
